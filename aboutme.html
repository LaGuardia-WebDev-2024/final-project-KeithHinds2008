Keith Hinds is a dynamic and versatile dancer, choreographer, and artist based in New York City. Currently attending Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School, Keith has demonstrated an exceptional range of skills across a wide array of dance genres. With training in everything from Contemporary, Ballet, and Hip Hop to Tap, Jazz, Vogue, West-African, and Modern, Keith is known for his ability to seamlessly blend multiple styles, showcasing his adaptability and creativity as a performer. His extensive training has earned him accolades such as being a 2024 YoungArts Winner in Modern/Contemporary Dance and receiving the Nuestro Futuro Award from Ballet Hispanico in 2023. Keith is also a dedicated student at MOVE|NYC’s Young Professionals Program, has done the Alvin Ailey Summer Intensive, trained at Ballet Hispanico, and the Juilliard Summer Intensive for 2 years in a row, where he further hones his craft under the mentorship of renowned figures in the dance world.

Keith’s professional experience spans major performances and events, including his recent role as a choreographer at Dance Lab New York's NextGen program, a dancer at the 2025 Dance Lab New York Honoree Gala and alongside Bishop Briggs in the Macy’s Thanksgiving Day Parade for Netflix’s Wednesday float. His stage presence has also graced venues such as the New York City Center for Dance Against Cancer and Radio City Music Hall, where him and his fellow young professionals from MOVE|NYC| opened the Christmas Spectacular for the Rockettes.

As a choreographer, Keith approaches movement as a powerful vessel for storytelling. His work explores the complexity of the human experience, striving to depict humanity in all its beauty, vulnerability, and resilience. Keith believes that true art emerges when we share stories that resonate—whether intimately personal or universally felt. Through choreography, he invites audiences not just to witness a performance, but to experience it with him, creating space for empathy, connection, and reflection.

His choreographic voice has been featured in Dance Lab NY’s NextGen Apprenticeship and Residency, as well as his original work Hesitant Harmony perfromed at LaGuardia High School's Rising Stars and MOVE|NYC|'s Winter Intensive showcase. With a deep-rooted commitment to authenticity and expression, Keith continues to evolve as both a performer and a maker, using dance to build bridges between people, stories, and worlds. Keith’s commitment to his craft, coupled with his versatility across diverse dance styles, sets him apart as a rising star in the dance world. With a passion for pushing boundaries and a deep dedication to both performance and creation, Keith is poised to make a significant impact in the industry.
